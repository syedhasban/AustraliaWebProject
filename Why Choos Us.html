Why choose Hosting Australia?

We could ramble on about our impeccable up-time record, that we provide 100% Australian support, or even the fact that with onshore servers, your information couldn’t be more secure. But the no-nonsense bottom line with us is that we’re here to completely remove any and all hassles that come with hosting your website – we work hard so you don’t have to.

Plus when you need to speak with us, whether via phone, email or live chat, instead of an annoying robotic response, it may surprise you that an actual human (based in Australia) is at the other end.

What happens to your
business when your
website goes offline?

Imagine arriving at your brick-and-mortar business one morning only to find the windows have been boarded up and the doors welded shut. There’s no way of anyone getting in – not you nor your customers.

The same concept can be applied to your online business when your site goes down. A flow-on effect occurs: your business becomes inaccessible, which leads to missed sales, a tarnished reputation and damaged customer relationships.

In a digital world, a constant online web presence is paramount. We’re committed to providing you 100% uptime so your business can continue operating as it should.

Whether you choose shared or cloud solutions, you can rest assured that your website is in the very best of hands.
