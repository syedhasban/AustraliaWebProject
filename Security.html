Security is one of your biggest selling points

Secure your business

Online security isn’t just important for eCommerce websites running credit card payments. Any site that requests personal information and contact details from users needs proper protection in place. Otherwise those customers may think twice about coming back.

Customer trust

When a customer enters their credit card details online, it shows they have a certain amount of faith in the business. To give your customers a total peace of mind when entering details on your site, whether it’s credit card or personal information, let Hosting Australia provide you with SSL security you can trust.

Our hosting experts have your security covered

Not all SSL certificates are the same. That’s why, when you’re in the market to secure your website with us, we’ll first discuss with you the scope of your business and ask a few questions to gauge what’s right for you. Once we’ve got all the information we need, we can then provide you with the certificate that fits your business best, delivering the right protection at the right price.

Like all technology out there, there may be something that comes up. Whatever the time of day (or night) you need us, we’re here for you to answer any questions and resolve any issues you may have, guaranteed.
